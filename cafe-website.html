Our Story

More Than Coffee — It's a Community

Born from a love of slow mornings and exceptional beans, Brewed & Co. has been a gathering place since 2018. We source directly from farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — ensuring every sip supports the people who grew it.
